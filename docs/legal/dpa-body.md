# Data Processing Addendum

This Data Processing Addendum applies when CompuShare processes personal data on behalf of a customer in connection with the CompuShare inference marketplace, private connector, billing, support, and operational security services.

## Roles

- The customer is the controller for customer data submitted through its organization account, unless the customer separately acts as a processor for its own end customers.
- CompuShare acts as a processor for customer data handled to provide the service.
- Subprocessors are listed in the appendices generated with each export.

## Subject matter and duration

CompuShare processes customer data only for the duration needed to provide the contracted services, enforce security controls, maintain service integrity, meter usage, reconcile billing, and satisfy lawful retention obligations.

## Nature and purpose of processing

Processing is limited to hosting, routing, serving, logging, billing, support, security monitoring, fraud review, and infrastructure operations required to deliver the service and its documented private execution modes.

## Categories of data subjects and personal data

Data subjects may include customer personnel, customer end users, and provider organization contacts where relevant to service delivery. Personal data may include account identifiers, contact details, prompts and outputs submitted by the customer, operational metadata, usage records, billing metadata, and support communications.

## Processor obligations

CompuShare will:

- process personal data only on documented customer instructions as reflected in the service configuration and applicable agreement;
- ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations;
- implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures;
- assist the customer with data subject requests, security incident response, and controller obligations to the extent required by applicable law and the service design;
- delete or return personal data at the end of service, subject to lawful retention requirements; and
- make available information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance with processor obligations.

## Subprocessors

CompuShare may engage subprocessors for defined service functions. CompuShare remains responsible for subprocessors to the extent required by applicable law and imposes written data protection obligations that are materially consistent with this addendum.

## International transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the originating jurisdiction, CompuShare will rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms described in the generated appendices and the governing agreement.
